Beijing Jiaotong University AI Education System

AI Empowers Educational Innovation

Integrating advanced large language models and intelligent retrieval technology to provide comprehensive intelligent assistance tools for educators and learners

Knowledge Base Q&A System

Based on Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) technology, it can quickly retrieve relevant information from existing documents and knowledge bases to generate accurate and coherent answers.

Suitable for course material queries, academic literature analysis, knowledge point organization, and other scenarios, making information retrieval and understanding more efficient.

Agent System

An intelligent agent based on the Thinking and Acting (ReAct) framework, capable of analyzing complex problems and executing a series of reasoning and operations to solve multi-step tasks.

Particularly suitable for complex problem analysis, programming assistance, research methodology guidance, and other scenarios requiring deep thinking.
